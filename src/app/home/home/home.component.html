About the Smart Campus Surveillance-Based Guidance System

The Smart Campus Surveillance-Based Guidance System is an innovative solution designed to enhance the safety and efficiency of university campuses. By integrating advanced technologies such as facial recognition, real-time video processing, and automated alert systems, this system provides a comprehensive approach to campus security and student management.

Key Objectives:

  • Enhanced Security: The system tracks only students with verified identities, significantly improving campus security and preventing unauthorized access.
  • Real-Time Monitoring: Utilizing strategically placed IP cameras, the system continuously monitors student movements, allowing for swift action in case of emergencies.
  • Guidance for Students: Students receive audio instructions to guide them back to their classrooms if they are found in unauthorized areas.
  • User-Friendly Navigation: The system offers intuitive navigation features, helping students manage their time better.
